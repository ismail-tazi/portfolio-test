About

I spend most days in that space between raw tables and real decisions — cleaning messy datasets, building models, and shipping dashboards that people actually open on a Monday morning. I care as much about a well-indexed query as I do about a well-calibrated model.

Before moving fully into data, I worked adjacent to the business teams I now support — which is probably why I default to asking "what decision does this change?" before I open a notebook.
